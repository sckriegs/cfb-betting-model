## How to Read This Report

- **Ranking**: Current team ranking (top 25 only)
- **DK Spread**: DraftKings point spread (positive = home favored)

- **O/U Pick**: Over or Under pick with confidence 1-10

- **Note**: Only DraftKings and FanDuel odds are used
